WHY NEW ZEALAND?

Breathtaking Nature-

80% of New Zealand's land is untouched wilderness, offering stunning landscapes from snow-capped mountains to geothermal wonders and golden beaches.

4 National Parks:

Tongariro, Te Urewera, Fiordland, and Aoraki/Mount Cook, all UNESCO World Heritage Sites, showcase the country's diverse natural beauty.

Adventure Playground-

Ranked #1 in the world for outdoor activities by Lonely Planet in 2023, New Zealand offers everything from bungee jumping and white-water rafting to hiking and skiing.

Over 30 Great Walks:

Explore diverse trails like the Milford Track, Tongariro Alpine Crossing, and Routeburn Track, showcasing stunning scenery and challenging terrains.

Warm Kiwi Welcome-

New Zealand consistently ranks high in global happiness surveys, with its friendly and welcoming population often referred to as "Kiwis."

Compact and Diverse-

Experience diverse landscapes and activities within short distances-from glaciers and mountains to beaches and vineyards, all easily accessible with efficient transportation networks.

Small country size-

New Zealand's compact nature allows you to cover a lot of ground during your visit, maximizing your adventure.

Sustainable Tourism Leader-

New Zealand prioritizes sustainable tourism practices, focusing on protecting its natural environment and cultural heritage.

NZ Visa Types

Visitor Visa: Explore New Zealand's wonders for up to 9 months.

NZeTA: Visa waiver for short stays (up to 3 months) from eligible countries.
