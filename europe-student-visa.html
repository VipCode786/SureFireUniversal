Types of Europe Student Visa

Schengen Visa

  • Temporary visa for up to 3 months, extendable for residency.

Long Stay Visa

  • For courses longer than 6 months, includes a residency permit.

Student Visa in Europe

  • Most common visa for pursuing specific courses at European universities.

Europe Student Visa Eligibility

  • No age restriction (17 years and above).
  • Minimum 50% academic score.
  • English proficiency test may be required by some countries.

Europe Study Visa Requirements

  • Completed visa application form.
  • Proof of meeting age requirements.
  • Documentation from a European university.
  • Proof of financial means.
  • Flight tickets.
  • Language proficiency proof.

Working in Europe

Part-time work allowed for students. Employment opportunities post-graduation.

Europe Student Visa Processing Time

2 to 6 months, varying by visa type.
