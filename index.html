Process

Step

01

Passport

Your passport must be valid for at least six months beyond your period of stay in the United States

Step

02

IELTS

The International English Language Testing System (IELTS) is designed to help you work, study or migrate to a country where English is the native language.

Step

03

Apply In University/Collage

Choose where you want to go and what you want to study.

Step

04

Obtain Offer Letter

This letter is issued by the employer after the candidate has been selected for the job following an interview.

Step

05

First-Year Tuition Deposit

This deposit is non-refundable and put towards a student's total fees owing for their Initial Term.

Step

06

GIC of CAD 10,000

A GIC of CAD 10,000 (approximately INR 5.95 lacs) acts as evidence of funds from the approved Canadian-based bank.

Step

07

Medical

The medical examination will include a medical history review, physical examination, chest X-ray and blood tests for syphilis.

Step

08

Other Visa Documents

Original passport with at least six months of remaining validity and with blank visa pages, a photocopy of the passport's data page and the photo page if it is separate.

Step

09

Apply for Visa

An applicant for a visa typically needs to complete an application form, which is usually available at the embassy website of the country to which they are travelling.

Step

10

Visa Approval

Approved – You have met all the qualifications and are approved to receive a visa.
