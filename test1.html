ELIGIBILITY PROCESS - (How Surefire Helps)

Step 1: Assess Eligibility

Free initial consultation: Our experts assess your situation and confirm eligibility.

Detailed guidance: We explain the requirements and answer your questions clearly.

Document checklist: We provide a personalized list of required documents based on your unique case.

Step 2: Gather Documents

Document review and assistance: We ensure your documents are complete, accurate, and meet IRCC standards.

Translation services: If needed, we offer professional translation of documents by certified translators.

Guidance on obtaining specific documents: We help you understand what documents are needed and how to acquire them efficiently.

Step 3: Sponsor Your Spouse

Application preparation: We assist you in filling out the sponsorship application forms accurately and efficiently.

Financial assessment: We guide you in demonstrating your financial ability to sponsor your spouse.

Communication with IRCC: We handle communication with IRCC on your behalf, ensuring timely responses and addressing any concerns.

Step 4: Your Spouse Applies for Permanent Residency

Application preparation: We guide your spouse through the application process and ensure all required documents are submitted correctly.

Travel arrangements: We assist with visa applications and travel logistics if your spouse is applying from abroad.

Interview preparation: If required, we provide mock interviews and coaching to help your spouse feel confident and prepared.

Step 5: Processing and Decision

Case monitoring: We track your application status and keep you informed of any updates or requirements.

Communication with IRCC: We represent you and your spouse before IRCC, effectively communicating on your behalf.

Addressing requests for additional information: We help you prepare thorough responses to any requests from IRCC.
